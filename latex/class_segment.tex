\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_segment}{Segment} class representing a graph segment. 

The equality comparison for segments does not follow the same rules as the greater-\/than and less-\/than comparisons. Two segments are considered equal if their sequences are identical, regardless of their IDs or other attributes. A segment is considered less than another if its dis is smaller, and then by id.
